Introduction

We are running this Gym for the last 5 years. The motive of this Gym is to provide better Health to the citizens. We have trained more than 100 of young students for wrestling and other sports. We believe in the motive of "Healthy mind lives in Healthy body. We are still working hard on the development of the Gym so that more and more people get the advantage of this gym.

Our Trainers

Arjun Singh : He's got 20 years of experience as a Gym Trainee. He was a Trainee of Randeep Singh (Professional Wrestler) for a long time. He himself is a Professional Wrestler and Kabaddi Player. He has also represented his team as captain of Haryana team in the Indian Kabaddi Tournament.

Sonam Chawla : She's is one of the best female trainee in the city. She has got an experience of more than 10 years as a trainer. She is an ex-wrestler and basketball player. She has won Silver Medal in the Indian Open Wrestling Tournament.
